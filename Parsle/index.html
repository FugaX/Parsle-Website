What is Parsle?

Parsle is a collaborative classroom-based application made for Monta Vista that bridges the communication gaps between students and teachers.

  • Schedule online or in-person meetings with your teachers.
  • Collaborate with students, teachers, or tutors in real time using easy to use tools.
  • Create live study sessions with your friends.

Features

Parsle functions by creating class calendars for each class you have in which students and/or teachers can input dates as to when they are available for meetings or study-sessions.

Inspiration

We noticed in our everyday school lives that many students(ourselves included) were afraid to talk to our teachers due to a lack of communication between both sides. With Parsle, students no longer have to worry about that with the simple tools it provides to help students and teachers alike with studying and communication.

Clients

As of now, Parsle is only going to be used by students attending Monta Vista High School as it is still under development and is not yet ready for a higher user-bandwidth.

Next Steps

Our next steps are to finish and officially launch Parsle for Monta Vista so that its' students can use it freely while also benefiting in their academics.
